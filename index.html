FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What is a dark pattern?
Dark patterns are deceptive UI tricks that manipulate users — like fake countdown timers, pre-ticked boxes, or hidden fees. They're illegal under UK consumer law.
Is my website data safe?
Yes. We only scan publicly visible content — the same thing any visitor sees. We don't store your data or share it with third parties.
How accurate is the scan?
Our AI checks 7 trust signals in real time. It catches the most common issues but we recommend a full manual audit for compliance-critical decisions.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal. No contracts, no hidden fees.
